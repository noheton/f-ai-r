## 2026-05-06 — First-draft manuscript distilled from prior repo
*Author:* claude-opus-4-7 (under direction of repo owner)

distilling the methodology and FAIR thinking of
`noheton/Obscurity-Is-Dead` (same author), with all IoT-security
domain content abstracted away. The framing of F(AI)²R is corrected
