# F(AI²)R

<p class="lede">FAIR research with AI in the loop, twice. A working paper and a reproducible writing pipeline, versioned together.</p>

`F(AI²)R` re-reads the FAIR principles for an era in which Large Language
Models participate in scholarly production. The "²" is deliberate: AI
appears **twice** in the loop — once as **Authoring assistant**, once as
**Auditor of provenance**. Both passes are captured in a PROV-O graph that
travels with the manuscript.

This site is generated from the same repository that produces the paper.
The manuscript, the provenance graph, and the logbook are primary
artifacts and are kept consistent at every commit.
