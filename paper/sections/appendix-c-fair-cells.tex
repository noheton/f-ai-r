\section{F(AI)\texorpdfstring{\textsuperscript{2}}{2}R Cell Mapping}
\label{sec:appendix-cell-mapping}
\label{app:fair-cells}

The original FAIR Guiding Principles enumerate fifteen sub-principles.
F(AI)\textsuperscript{2}R adds, for each, an authoring-pass cell and
an audit-pass cell that name the artefact involved. The full list is
in \texttt{doc/fair.md}; Table~\ref{tab:cells} is a condensed view.

\begin{table}[!ht]
\centering
\small
\begin{tabular}{lp{4.6cm}p{4.6cm}}
\toprule
\textbf{Sub-principle} & \textbf{Authoring pass (AI\textsubscript{1})} & \textbf{Audit pass (AI\textsubscript{2})} \\
\midrule
F1 (identifier)       & Mint Zenodo DOI; assign \texttt{fair2r:Claim} IRIs. & Verify identifier resolves; verify IRIs are unique. \\
F2 (rich metadata)    & Populate CFF / CodeMeta / Zenodo metadata. & Verify metadata surfaces agree. \\
F3 (metadata link)    & Reference the data identifier from metadata. & Verify the link is bidirectional. \\
F4 (registered)       & Deposit at Zenodo; minimum DataCite metadata. & Verify deposit visible from the registry. \\
A1 (retrievable)      & Public Git repo; static site. & Verify clone-and-build succeeds on a clean machine. \\
A1.1 (open protocol)  & HTTPS / Git. & --- \\
A1.2 (auth where needed) & Document redaction policy and consent gate. & Verify redacted markers cover the documented taxonomy. \\
A2 (metadata persists) & Metadata in Git history; immutable Zenodo deposit. & Verify post-retraction metadata still reachable. \\
I1 (formal language)  & Turtle for provenance; \LaTeX{} for prose; CFF / JSON for metadata. & Validate Turtle; lint CFF. \\
I2 (FAIR vocab)       & PROV-O + DCTERMS + FOAF + \texttt{fair2r:}. & Verify namespaces stable. \\
I3 (qualified refs)   & \texttt{prov:wasDerivedFrom}, \texttt{fair2r:contradicts}, etc. & Verify graph has no dangling refs. \\
R1 (multiple attrs)   & Record \texttt{fair2r:VerificationState}, \texttt{prov:wasAttributedTo}, etc. & Verify rung distribution at submission time. \\
R1.1 (clear licence)  & MIT for code/RDF; CC-BY-4.0 for prose. & Verify SPDX expressions. \\
R1.2 (provenance)     & The provenance graph itself. & Replay the graph; report drift. \\
R1.3 (community std)  & Follow FAIR4RS / FAIR4ML where applicable. & Cross-check against the relevant standard at audit time. \\
\bottomrule
\end{tabular}
\caption{F(AI)\textsuperscript{2}R cell mapping. Authoritative source:
\texttt{doc/fair.md} in the public repository.}
\label{tab:cells}
\end{table}

The table is a contract. Adopting F(AI)\textsuperscript{2}R amounts to
producing an audit pass artefact for every cell in the right column at
submission time, and to declaring the residual gaps explicitly when an
artefact does not yet exist.
