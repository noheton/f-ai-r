Each practice below is individually unoriginal. The argument is
that adopting any in isolation is insufficient against the failure
modes of \S\ref{sec:failure-modes}, while adopting all eight ---
with the pipeline of \S\ref{sec:pipeline} enforcing the seams ---
is sufficient against most and honest about the rest.
Figure~\ref{fig:failure-mode-coverage} reads the eight as columns
against the failure modes as rows.

\begin{description}[leftmargin=1.2em,itemsep=2pt]
    \item[1. Transcript preservation.] Every session touching the
        manuscript exports its transcript under
        \texttt{doc/transcripts/}, bound via \texttt{prov:used} on
        the authoring activity and typed
        \texttt{fair2r:Transcript}.
    \item[2. Verification-status labelling
        (\S\ref{sec:ladder}).] Every claim carries one of the five
        FSM rungs in the graph (not the prose); invoking lower than
        the evidence permits is allowed, higher is a defect.
    \item[3. Per-claim provenance maps.] Every named claim has a
        \texttt{fair2r:Claim} IRI with at minimum
        \texttt{prov:wasGeneratedBy}, \texttt{prov:wasAttributedTo},
        \texttt{prov:wasDerivedFrom}, and
        \texttt{fair2r:verificationState}; retraction is a
        \texttt{prov:Invalidation}, never a deletion. This is the
        structured-documentation descendant of Datasheets for
        Datasets~\cite{gebru2021datasheets} and Model
        Cards~\cite{mitchell2019modelcards} at claim granularity.
    \item[4. Mirror discipline (\S\ref{sec:handback}).] Every
        primary artefact has a (source, render) pair; the build
        either agrees or fails. \emph{Asset honesty over asset
        completeness}: placeholders are allowed but must visibly
        identify themselves as AI-authored and pending.
    \item[5. Recursive meta-process documentation
        (\S\ref{sec:evolution}).] A paper prescribing a discipline
        must have been written under it. The manuscript, prompts,
        graph, logbook, and pipeline are all public; the
        cooperation that produced them is narrated in
        \S\ref{sec:evolution} and Appendix~\ref{sec:appendix-evolution}.
    \item[6. Base-rate-anchored AI disclosure.] Disclosure is
        anchored to numeric base rates for the failure modes the
        rungs mitigate~\cite{walters2023fabrication,magesh2024legal};
        the fraction of claims at each rung is declared at
        submission time.
    \item[7. Legal honesty about authorship.] AI is acknowledged
        but is not an author. The position holds across Germany
        (\S2 \emph{UrhG}), the United States, and the major medical
        journals~\cite{icmje2023}: AI cannot hold copyright, consent
        to publication, or be held accountable. The
        \texttt{CITATION.cff} lists humans only.
    \item[8. FAIR alignment as precondition.] \emph{Do not retrofit
        FAIR.} The eight practices on day one supply the four axes
        at low marginal cost; at submission time the manuscript has
        to be re-decomposed into claims, transcripts reconstructed,
        rungs assigned in retrospect at maximum verification
        overhead.
\end{description}

\noindent The integration is the contribution.
