## Caption (one sentence)
The dual loop the squared notation encodes: an authoring agent (left,
blue) writes into the manuscript bundle and an audit agent (right,
green) reads it back, with defects flowing through a dashed handback
registry rather than through in-place edits.

## Data / relations the figure must show
- A left-hand "AI as writer" loop: drafts prose, proposes claims,
  mints identifiers; self-loop indicating iterative drafting.
- A right-hand "AI as reader" loop: verifies citations, validates
  the graph, files defects; self-loop indicating iterative review.
- A central manuscript bundle: prose, sources, prompts, transcripts,
  graph.
- A "writes" arrow from writer into the bundle (blue).
- A "reads" arrow from the bundle into the reader (green).
- A dashed yellow "handback (defect registry)" edge from reader back
  to writer, going under the bundle.
