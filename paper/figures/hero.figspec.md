## Caption (one sentence)
The F(AI)\textsuperscript{2}R hero graphic: the manuscript bundle
sits as the shared substrate between an authoring pass that writes
into it and an audit pass that reads it back, encoding the central
claim that LLMs participate in scholarly writing both as participants
and as readers.

## Data / relations the figure must show
- The five artefact classes the paper argues are produced by
  LLM-assisted writing: claims, sources, prompts, transcripts, and
  the provenance graph.
- The (AI)\textsubscript{1} authoring pass arching over the bundle
  with five down-arrows into each artefact (writes).
- The (AI)\textsubscript{2} audit pass arching under the bundle with
  five up-arrows from each artefact (reads).
- A right-edge synthesis showing the typographic commitment
  $\text{F}(\text{AI})^{2}\text{R}$ with the gloss "two passes
  through every artefact".
- Top-edge tagline: "LLMs as participants and as readers --- the
  (AI) factor multiplied through FAIR."

## Medium
TikZ. No raster, no rounded corners, no shadow effects. DLR Corporate
Design palette only: `dlrBlau1`, `dlrBlau5`, `dlrGruen1`,
`dlrGruen5`, `dlrGrau1`, `dlrGrau3`, `dlrGrau5`, `dlrAccent`.
Hairline rules at 0.3--0.8\,pt.
